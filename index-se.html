Technologies

OpenStack

The leading Open Source Infrastructure As A Service, allowing companies to deploy web-scale infrastructure behind the firewall. OpenStack has a huge industry following and has more than 9,500 individual members from 100 countries and 850 different organizations

CloudFoundry

Spun out of Vmware along with its mothership Pivotal, the open source CloudFoundry project has gathered the most momentum in the PaaS market and is well positioned to come out ahead as the market matures.

Docker

Docker is a brilliant technology allowing mere-mortal developers to wrap any software stack in a container that will run anywhere and that can be scaled and managed as one unit.

Amazon Web Services

Amazon pioneered the Infrastructure As A Service, and is now the big gorilla in the market. Amazon Web Services is a portfolio of cloud computing services, of which Amazon EC2 and Amazon S3 are the best known.
